I am a PhD student in Genetics at Professor Jian-Min Zhou's laboratory, where I investigate the evolutionary interplay between plant immunity and crop domestication. My research integrates pan-genome architectures, NLRome profiling, and functional genomics to decode evolutionary trajectories of disease resistance mechanisms across major crops.
